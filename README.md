## How to Use

1. **Load Models**: Click "Load Files" or "Load Folder" to upload 3D brain scan files (.glb or .gltf)
2. **Enable AR Mode**: Click the camera icon in the top-right to activate augmented reality
3. **Draw Annotations**: Use the pen tool at the bottom to draw on the models
4. **Manage Layers**: Use the layers panel to toggle visibility of different brain segments
